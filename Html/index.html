Social Impact of AI & GenAI

Exploring how intelligent systems reshape creativity, labour, and society.


"The digital world, labour, and creativity are being completely transformed by AI and generative AI. In the present project, This project looks into their social impacts, ensuring the opportunities are taken into consideration against the burden of responsibility."



About

Understanding the Rise of AI & GenAI


"In essence, AI simply allows computers to perform tasks at which human intelligence is required. With regards to generative AI, it may even create brand new text, pictures, even code.

These tools keep growing in size, and as such, they are intervening in the processes of creativity, work, and communication, not to mention that it erodes problems such as prejudice, privacy, and who is holding whom accountable."
